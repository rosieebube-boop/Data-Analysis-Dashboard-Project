# Data-Analysis-Dashboard-Project
## Project
I used the Sales Dataset of a random company to create visualizations using Ms Excel, Tableau and Power BI. The Dataset involes information about the companies sales from 2023-2025.

## Questions(KPI's)
-Best selling product.
-Compare the revenue per year.
-Which state has the most orders?
-Compare the profit per year.
-Which states generates the most revenue?

## Process
Cleaned the Dataset on MS Excel
Formated the Excel sheet and created a Profit column
Created Pivot Tables and chart and inserted slicers to make it more interactive
Uploaded the new dataset to Tableau and Power BI
Created the Dasboards on Tableau and Power BI

## Project Insights and Final Conclusions
The total Revenue in 2023 was $6,714,010.00, in 2024 it was $6,404,800.00 and in 2025 it was $7,158,410.00. The best selling product category in the three years was Clothing. The most profit was made in 2025. In 2023 the highest profit was made in April, in 2024 the highest profit was made in April and in 2025 the highest profit was made around October.
